PRIVATE EVENTS

	  
	  The Jaipur room is a 60-seat open-air room with 18-foot high ceilings
	  framed by a 200-year-old handcarved wooden arch salvaged form a
	  palace in Jaipur. Decorated with Burmese teak wooden tables, the room
	  discretely overlooks main dining room and can be made subtly private 
	  with a simple drawing of sheer curtains.
     
	 
          The concept of Junoon encompasses the needs of discriminating diners
	   who are searching for a uniquely transcendent dining experience.
	   Junoon's private dining areas offer the opportunity to enjoy the heights
	   of hospitality with custom designed menus tailor made for your special
	   needs.
       
	   
           Please contact Manager for further information on Junoon's private
	   dining room and for consultation on customized menus with cocktail
	   pairings.
