PATIALA LOUNGE



	  The patiala lounge is named after bhpinder singh of patiala, an iconic
	  maharaja who is well known for his flamboyant extravagance. His 
	  insatiable love for food and libations.
     
	 
	 
          The patiala lounge presents contemporary cocktails that crossover
	  many of the herbs and spices found in cuisine. We also offer extensive
	  selection of artisanal spirits that can be used to prepare many of the
	  favourite classic spanning the century.	  
       
	   
	   
          The full ala carte menu and tasting menu's are always available, in
	  addition to a lounge menu of small bites and stuffed breads.
